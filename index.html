Our Story

More Than a Haircut.
A Ritual.

Chirag Salon was founded on a single belief: every person deserves to walk out feeling like the best version of themselves. Since 2012, our team of trained stylists has been combining old-school grooming craft with contemporary design sensibility.

We don't rush appointments. We don't cut corners. We read the room — whether you want a sharp executive look, a relaxed textured style, or a bold reinvention — and we deliver it with consistency.

Precision Every line, every edge, intentional.
Luxury Premium products, unhurried pace.
